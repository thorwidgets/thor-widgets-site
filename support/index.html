Support

Contact

Before Contacting Support

To help us resolve your issue faster, please include:

  • The email address used for your purchase
  • Your license key if relevant
  • A description of the issue
  • Any error messages or screenshots that may help

Common Requests

  • License activation problems
  • Moving your license to a new device
  • Missing license keys
  • Purchase or payment issues
  • Technical issues with the application

License Transfers

If you need to use your license on a new computer, one device rebind is available free of charge.

Additional device changes can be requested through support and will be reviewed case-by-case.

Response Time

Support is provided on a best-effort basis. We aim to respond as soon as reasonably possible.
