3. Information We Collect

License and Purchase Information

When you purchase a license, we may collect and process:

  • Email address
  • License key
  • License status
  • Purchase date
  • Purchase amount
  • Stripe Customer ID
  • Stripe Session ID
  • License activation information
  • Device identifier used for license verification
  • License rebind information

Device Information

To prevent unauthorized sharing of licenses, Thor Widgets uses a device identifier for license activation and verification.

This information is only used to ensure that a license is used according to its terms. It is not used for advertising, tracking, or profiling.

Anonymous Usage Analytics

We may collect anonymous usage statistics about how features and widgets are used inside the application.

This may include information such as which widgets are opened and how frequently they are used.

This information is not connected to your email address, license key, payment information, or device identifier.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Improve the application through anonymous analytics

Payments are processed through Stripe.

Thor Widgets does not store complete payment card information. Stripe may process payment information and transaction details required to complete purchases and provide payment services.

For more information about how Stripe processes personal data, please refer to Stripe's own privacy documentation.
